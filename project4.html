Fitness App

Motivation Made Simple.

The new year often starts with energy and good intentions, but motivation fades quickly. This app helps you stay focused with simple reminders and supportive features that keep your goals clear. It turns resolutions into lasting habits, not just January promises.

Client
School project
Services
UI & UX Design
User Research
Type
App
Date
February 2025

The concept started with a simple goal our group set for 2025: going to the gym more often. Staying motivated isn’t always easy, so we came up with an app that helps us stay on track while making the whole experience more enjoyable. It’s not meant to feel like a strict coach, but more like a playful motivator that gives you just the right nudge to keep going.

A big part of this is the badge and reward system. You earn badges as you make progress, creating a light sense of friendly competition within the group. It’s not about being the best — it’s about celebrating progress together and giving each other that extra bit of encouragement. Making it fun also makes it easier to stick with.

You also get a personal dashboard that gives you a clear overview of how you’re doing. No overwhelming data or complicated charts — just a clean, simple place to see what you’ve achieved and what you’re working toward. It feels like a calm starting point you can check in with every day.

Community plays an important role as well. You can talk with others, ask questions, share experiences, or drop a bit of motivation when someone needs it. Combined with personalized notifications and widgets you can set yourself, your goals stay visible and within reach. Together, these elements turn working out into something you don’t just have to do, but something you want to do — and something you can keep growing in, step by step.

By combining playful rewards, clear progress tracking, social support, and personalized reminders, the app transforms going to the gym from a chore into a motivating and enjoyable habit. It doesn’t just help us stick to our 2025 goal — it makes the whole journey more engaging, social, and fun, so staying active becomes something we actually look forward to.
