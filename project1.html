Playdora

Immersive Shopping Experience

Playdora is an interactive online store that brings Ancient Greek toys to life. I helped design and develop the platform, combining e‑commerce with storytelling and augmented reality.

At Playdora, I contributed to creating an interactive online store for toys inspired by Ancient Greece. I helped shape the platform so that users could not only purchase toys but also explore the history and stories behind them.

I focused on designing an immersive shopping experience where collecting toys feels like visiting a virtual museum. Users can view the historical background of each toy through AR and learn about the cultural origins of the designs.

In addition, I worked on the user-friendly and responsive e‑commerce functionality. I designed the catalog, product pages, and shopping cart flow to ensure a smooth and intuitive shopping experience while keeping the storytelling aspect intact.

My role combined design and front-end development, making sure the digital experience was both visually engaging and technically robust.

The Playdora project showed me how education, storytelling, and e‑commerce can come together in a single platform. I’m proud to have been part of this team and to have contributed to an experience that creatively combines learning and shopping.
